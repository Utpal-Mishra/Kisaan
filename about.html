About

SWASTHYA is a healthcare website intended to decipher, analyze and create awareness with complete statistics of disease in India (i.e. within States and Cities) from its Spread, Active Areas, Affected Age-Groups with relevant Healthcare Centres - Hospitals and Clinic - for Booking Appointment and Treatment of a user in Close Proximity via Geo-location.

The Web Application will be useful Active Alert Notification whenever a patient is recorded for a disease in the neighbour, along with providing details on the symptoms, cases and remedies for a dieases.

*The application is still in a Developing Phrase so, many components will be revamped and incorporated with time.
